What is Sports Analytics?+

Sports analytics is the systematic application of statistical and mathematical methods to sports data. It provides teams, coaches, and management with insights that drive better decision-making on and off the field.

In a modern sporting environment, every pass, sprint, and decision is tracked. By analyzing these data points, we can predict player performance, optimize game strategies, and minimize injury risks. It's the "Moneyball" evolution applied to the digital age.

What is this website?+

This website contains personal projects that focus on what happens on and off the basketball court. Statistical data, patterns and solutions are going to be showcased here, analyzing different teams' play style.

The content that is uploaded, is only about showcasing personal work. It has no purpose of critisizing or diminishing any coach's game style, or any professional's work.

Learn about the EuroLeague+

The EuroLeague stands as the pinnacle of European basketball, where 18 elite teams battle from September to May for the ultimate trophy. Top-class athletes compete on the biggest stage, each striving to lead their organization to the highest point of the pedestal.

Since its founding season in 2000-2001, legendary names have graced the court, taking the league to new heights. Fast forward to today, the EuroLeague has evolved into one of the most grueling basketball competitions worldwide. With expanding schedules and skyrocketing budgets, the margin for error has never been thinner.

In such a high-stakes environment where a single round can shift the entire standings, traditional intuition is no longer enough. This project leverages sports analytics to navigate the complexity of this "marathon" and provides a deep dive into how challenging and volatile a single month can be for even the top-tier teams.

In an era where the smallest margin can decide a championship, analytics provides the "Sixth Man" every team needs to stay ahead of the competition.
