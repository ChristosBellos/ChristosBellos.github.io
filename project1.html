Olympiacos & Panathinaikos January Analysis

In this project I focus on analyzing in-game stats for two Greek basketball teams, Olympiacos and Panathinaikos. The stats that are kept focus on real time data, in teams' offense and defense. This project's main purpose is to find trends and reasons behind each team's results and figure out, if necessery, a solution to their negative outcome.
