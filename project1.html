Olympiacos & Panathinaikos January Analysis

The EuroLeague has evolved into one of the most grueling basketball competitions worldwide. With expanding schedules and skyrocketing budgets, the margin for error has never been thinner. In such a high-stakes environment where a single round can shift the standings, traditional intuition isn't enough. This project leverages sports analytics to navigate the complexity of the EuroLeague 'marathon' and gives a small taste of how challenging a single month can be for some teams.

In this project I focus on analyzing in-game stats for two Greek basketball teams, Olympiacos and Panathinaikos, for the month of January. The stats that are kept focus on real time data, in teams' offense and defense. This project's main purpose is to find trends and reasons behind each team's results and figure out, if necessery, a solution to their negative outcome.
