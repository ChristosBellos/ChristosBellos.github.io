Olympiakos Piraeus January Analysis

EuroLeague: A Data-Driven Deep Dive

Overview

The EuroLeague stands as the pinnacle of European basketball, where 18 elite teams battle from September to May for the ultimate trophy. Top-class athletes compete on the biggest stage, each striving to lead their organization to the highest point of the pedestal.

Since its founding season in 2000-2001, legendary names have graced the court, taking the league to new heights. Fast forward to today, the EuroLeague has evolved into one of the most grueling basketball competitions worldwide. With expanding schedules and skyrocketing budgets, the margin for error has never been thinner.

In such a high-stakes environment where a single round can shift the entire standings, traditional intuition is no longer enough. This project leverages sports analytics to navigate the complexity of this "marathon" and provides a deep dive into how challenging and volatile a single month can be for even the top-tier teams.

Project Analysis: January – February Deep Dive

This project examines the performance of Olympiacos and Panathinaikos in the EuroLeague, covering all matches from January 6, 2026, to February 6, 2026. Our goal is to dissect every possession to uncover the tactical trends of the Greek giants.

🏀 Offensive End Metrics

Detailed tracking of scoring efficiency and shot creation:

  • Offense Second: Seconds taken to execute the offense.
  • Time Zone: The specific "Time Zone" of each shot attempt.
  • C/U: Contested, slightly contested, or uncontested attempts.
  • 2p/3p: 2-pointers vs. 3-pointers.
  • Made/Missed: Shooting results (Made/Missed/Foul).
  • Off. Reb. After Miss: Offensive rebounds secured.
  • Who Took It (O.R.): Identifying the responsible player for the rebound.
  • Shot Created: Method of shot creation (10 distinct categories).
  • Player Of Interest: Key involved players.

🛡️ Defensive End Metrics

Analysis of defensive schemes and effectiveness:

  • Screen Defense: Type of defense applied on opponent screens.
  • Player: Player defending screen.
  • Basket Allowed: Tracking allowed shots vs. not allowed ones.
  • No Shot Result: Turnover, steals, fouls, etc.
  • C/U: Contest levels on allowed shots.
  • 2p/3p: 2-pointers vs. 3-pointers.
  • Made/Missed: Shooting results (Made/Missed/Foul).
